Sprinkle Safari is an adventure/role-playing game. You control a young elephant on a quest to defeat the evil Dr. Llama and reclaim the world's donuts.

Meet Dr. Llama in his lair by adventuring through 50 levels in 5 different worlds and battling the cute and wacky cast of 40 animals, from dogs to bears to dinosaurs. Collect the most powerful animals to defeat the evil minions of Dr. Llama!

Not only can you journey through the wide world in adventure mode - you can test your battle skills and get to meet new animals with Minigames, which are quick 5-on-5 death matches.
